Corporate Members

Corporate members are the trustee members who steer this organization towards a sustainable future as well as by making sure this nonprofit organization has adequate resources to advance its mission. These members are responsible for maintaining the group’s dignity and robustness by influencing the newly added members. Despite of their own schedules, they do not hesitate in organizing training sessions for the student members and imparting knowledge they have gained from their own experiences. They bring a lot to the table and maintain and unwavering interest to the achieve the goals of the organization.

Student Members

Student members are those who are the future of the organization and are under training for keeping up the group’s strength. They play an important role in strengthening this group. The simple fact is united we stand, divided we fall. This group is manifestation of that united existence, and student members are the part of this organization.
